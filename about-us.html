About Us

We're a community driven 5-a-side league based in Fake Place, England. Our main focus is bringing together people of all ages, backgrounds, and beliefs to a safe enviroment where they can indulge there need for competitive football. We aim to provide the opportunity for people to build new friendships and have fun.


Who is allowed to join?

When we say everyone we mean everyone. We have leagues for boys, girls and leagues with mixed teams. We run a league for ages 5-8, 9-12, 13-15 and 16+. We also run a league for over 50s. So there's no excuses for you to not get involved.


How does it work?

The league runs for a total of 12 weeks twice a year. From Sep 1 - Dec 1 and Mar 1- Jun 1. You'll play 3 teams a week on you're leagues specific day (see league timings below) and the top 4 teams with the most wins by the final week will go into a knockout tournament to see who wins the league and the bragging rights.
