About Us

We're a community driven 5-a-side league based in Leighton Buzzard, England. Our main focus is bringing together people of all ages, backgrounds, and beliefs to a safe enviroment where they can indulge there need for competitive football. We aim to provide the opportunity for people to build new friendships and have fun.
